## Project Overview
This project involved analyzing a dataset of over 500 Disney movies released between 1937 and 2016. The goal was to visualize how different genres have performed over time and how inflation has impacted the perception of "box office success."

## Key Insights
* **Genre Dominance:** Musical and Adventure films consistently show the highest average adjusted gross.
* **The Inflation Factor:** Comparing original gross to inflation-adjusted gross reveals the true scale of success for early classics like *Snow White*.
* **Rating Distribution:** A clear breakdown of movie counts by MPAA rating shows Disney's historical focus on 'G' and 'PG' rated content.

## Methodology
The data was cleaned and transformed using Excel before being imported into Tableau. I utilized calculated fields to handle inflation adjustments and designed a 2x2 grid dashboard to provide a high-level executive summary on a single screen.
